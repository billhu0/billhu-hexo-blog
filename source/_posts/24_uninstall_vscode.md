## How to uninstall vscode thoroughly on macOS


- Delete settings and configurations

    ```shell
    rm -rf $HOME/Library/Application\ Support/Code/
    ```

- Delete plugins

    ```shell
    rm -rf $HOME/.vscode/
    ```

- And then delete 'vscode.app' from 'Applications'
